About Us

SHPE is a nationally established organization with 10,000+ members that has supported Hispanics and non-Hispanics through their academic and professional endeavors in STEM. SHPE at SMU serves the same mission of changing lives by empowering the Hispanic community to realize it’s fullest potential and to impact the world through STEM awareness, access, support, and development.
