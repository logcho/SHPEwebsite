JOIN US

To join SHPE SMU as an official member, there are two key steps in the process. The first step is to create a SHPE Connect account and pay your $10 National membership fee. There are a few key steps you need to follow during the creation of your SHPE Connect account, so please reference the SHPE Connect 101 documentation we created just for you!

NEW MEMBERSHIP

renew membership

shpe connect 101

The second step of becoming an official SHPE SMU member is to join SHPE through Connect.SMU. To do so, you will need to click the button on the right labeled 'Join SHPE SMU Connect.' After clicking this button, you should be taken directly to the SHPE SMU Connect Page. Then, you will need to locate the 'Join' button which should be located at the top right corner of the page. Click on that button, and you should be all set!
