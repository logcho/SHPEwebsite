Julie Tapia is a Senior studying computer science in the Lyle School of Engineering.

She also wants to inspire and motivate young Latinos to pursue a higher education. As the CFO, she has secured funding for SHPE SMU to attend conferences and host professional events. Julie enjoys reading, running, playing soccer, and trying new food. For any questions, reach out to her via email.
