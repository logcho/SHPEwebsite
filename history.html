SHPE History

2005: SHPE at Southern Methodist University was founded as a vision by our Hispanic Student Advisor to bring Hispanic professional organization to campus. The SHPE SMU chapter was finally established in 2005 and some time later, due to lack of compliance, lost charter.

2018: SHPE SMU was put under new management and the president at the time set out to restructure the chapter. In the beginning, a budget could not be requested and basic organizational activities could not be done. Fortunately, charter was regained in Fall 2018 through the hard work of the executive team.

After this accomplishment, SHPE SMU hosted its first series of events since 2005 and worked arduously to establish relationships with other organizations. The first year of reorganization ended with a variety of accomplishments, lessons, and experiences.

2019: The work done and structure created in 2018 allowed for the chapter to come into fruition. For the first time, students were able to attend Nationals through SHPE SMU. Additionally, through diligent work the executive board aimed to build new relations with SHPE DFW, SHPE National, Fortune 100 companies, SMU professors, as well as local professionals. Due to the hard work and engagement, SHPE SMU was awarded the Blue Chip Chapter Award in Spring 2020.
